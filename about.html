About Us

Our Misson - a 100% invasive free Ohio

Educating one citizen at a a time for a cleaner world

The Ohio Plant Authority is a not-for-profit organization with one goal - to eradicate invasive species in our community. Based out of Cincinnati, Ohio, we provide citizens with information on local invasive plants, tailored to their region. This includes detailed identification guides, as well as articles on prevention and removal.
